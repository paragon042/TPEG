Description

Synopsis | A group of friends who each have a struggle of their own was forced to discover all about it when a tragic incident occurred that changed everyone's lives. Incident that left their hearts scarred, but left a strengthened faith in their souls.

"This title reflects the theme of the film, which explores the hidden struggles that people face in their lives, and how they deal with them. The characters in the film all have different struggles that they are facing, and these struggles are not always visible to others. The title "Struggles Unfold" captures the idea that everyone has their own struggles, and that we should be more empathetic and understanding towards others, even if we can't always see what they are going through."
