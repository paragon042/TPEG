Filipino Myth Cosplay

Lariesh

"Hanan"

"Hanan, the second daughter of Bathala, the Supreme God, and sister to Mayari, is one of the celestial court of the Philippine mythology. Like the rising sun, this endearing deity gleaming with golds, symbolizes rebirth and new beginning.""

Marco

"Apolaki"

"Apolaki is the god of sun and war revered by the Tagalog and Pangasinan people of the Philippines prior to colonization. According to a myth, he was the son of the supreme god of being Bathala and a mortal woman, along with his sister Mayari, the goddess of the moon."
